Our Process

Our Process-

How It Works


At Bathrooms Made Eazy, we strive to make the renovation process as easy as possible for our clients. We understand that any home renovation can be stressful, so we meticulously organise and plan the entire project to ensure it runs quickly and efficiently, minimising disruption. Our process generally takes 5 working days to complete a standard bathroom renovation, and during this time, we ensure your home remains clean.

  • During our initial consultation, we will visit you to discuss your ideas for your bathroom. We will provide catalogues and explore design options with you in the comfort of your own home.

  • After our consultation, we will design the perfect bathroom based on your requirements and provide a quotation. Once you approve the final design, we require a 10% deposit to begin, 60% within 3 days of commencing, and the remaining balance upon completion.

  • We will project manage the installation process from start to finish. Before any work begins, we will have a skip delivered for the removal of your old bathroom and waste. Protective material will be laid down to keep your flooring pristine. Typically, we strip out the old bathroom within the first day, then proceed to the first fix plumbing and electrical work. After this, the walls are prepped for tiling, which usually takes 1-2 days to complete. Following the tiling, we install all sanitary ware, units, lighting, and accessories.

  • Once the installation process is complete, we will clean everything up, polish your new tiles, and remove all job-related materials.
